Ha? Laman

iACADEMY 48 Hour Unity Game Jam

2020 | PC | UI/UX Developer | Unity & C#

Ha? Laman is a bomberman-style game for the PC played by two players using controllers. Players either play as a construction worker or a farmer, where the main goal of the game is to place the most trees or buildings after three rounds to win the game.

My Last Braincell

Global Game Jam 2020

Project Link

2020 | PC | Game Developer | Unity & C#

My Last Braincell is a 2D casual arcade game where you must reconnect all of your disconnected brain cells with your last working brain cell to get your brain up and running again. You have to be careful, however, as wandering cells can destroy all of your neuron connections! You have 20 seconds. And one braincell.

Re: Vision

Thesis 2

2019 | PC | Game & UI Developer | Unity & C#

KnockOut!

iACADEMY Bullyproof Campaign 2017-2018

2017 | Android & Web | UI/UX Design & Art, Front-End Web Developer | Adobe Photoshop, HTML & CSS

The KnockOut! App was developed by iACADEMY-SEAL's Officers. This app was developed for iACADEMY's Bullyproof Campaign for AY 2017-2018. During the event, participants were being taught various boxing routines, and the android app monitored their movements as a representation of their 'move' towards a bullyproof school. The web app receives all the data from the mobile app, and displays the total move count of the participants on the website.

iACADEMY Library System

iACADEMY-SEAL Scholars' Project

2018 | PC | UI/UX Art & Design | Adobe Photoshop

The iACADEMY Library System is a project developed by iACADEMY-SEAL's Scholars for the 3rd term of AY 2017-2018. The Library System features a Login for both admins and library users. On the admin side, administrators may view who visits the library frequently, and a rundown of the visits via the statistical data. On the library user side, users are able to log the time they spend in the library.
